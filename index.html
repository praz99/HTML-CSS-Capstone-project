How It Works

bookeeper works in four simple steps.

1. search

It is simple. On bookeeper homepage, in the search field, enter the name of the book you are looking for and click on search.
Alternately, click on the 'BOOKS' on the navigation bar on top of the page. You will see the full list of books.

2. select

Now you have the list of books, you can browse the books located under different categories. If you are still not sure about a book, you can read the description about the book, see the review and rating about the book to help you.

3. Request

Once you have found a book of your choice, you can now click on 'Request book'. This will lead you to a simple form asking about your details. Please fill up the form properly with correct details and submit it.

4. Read

Now you have your favourite book from your favourite writer in your hand, you can forget everything, and start reading.
